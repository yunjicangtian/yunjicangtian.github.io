时间状语从句

  • when=while=as:当什么的时候
    当while 翻译为此的时候，后面一定跟进行时态
  • before
  • after
  • since:自从，主句必用现在完成时
    I have fallen in love with you since i met you.
  • the moment=as soon as:一什么就
  • by the time: 到什么为止
  • until: 直到。。。才
  • not…until: 直到。。。才
  1. 状语从句的时态问题
    eg:
  • When love comes, l will hold her hands.
  • When love came, l failed to hold her hands.
  1. 区别until和not..until
  • l will wait here until you come.
  • l will not leave until you come. I didn’trealize the greatness of mothers until i grew up.
  1. 省略
    when i was a kid, i enjoyed singing songs.

地点状语从句

常用引导词：where

  • The kind of trees grow well in moist places. in moist places = where it is moist
  • 做地点状语的通常都是介词短语，很少是从句。

原因状语从句

  1. 常规引导词
  2. 连词
  3. 介词短语

eg:

  • He had a car accident because he was carless.(状语随便放)
  • He had a car accident for was careless.(连词必须放中间，放前面或后面和空气连吗？)
  • He had a car accident because of his carelessness.(介词后面必须跟名词)

目的状语从句

  1. 动词不定式 动词不定式表目的，后面跟动词原形
  2. 引导词
  • We will ascend higher in order to overlook farther.(动词不定式表目的)
  • further 表示抽象的远，比如心与心之间的距离远。
  • In order that we overlook farther we will ascend higher.(状语从句引导词)

结果状语从句

很简单，和汉语语序完全一样

eg:
It feels so hot today that i wear my dress.
My mother proves so kind that se is respected by all her staff.

很有用

  • so…that(so是副词，后跟形容词)=very
  • 只要在作文中见到名词，都可以有意识的加上同位语.

让步状语从句(虽然…尽管)

一种委婉的语气，话不说满，往后退一步

引导词

while

三个意思：

  • 翻译为但是，放句中。

  • 翻译为当…时候，后面必须跟进行时态。

    as的部分倒装

  • As i seem ugly, i keep gentle.

  • Ugly i seem, i keep gentel.
    as 引导的让步状语从句，从句的表语可以放在句首构成部分倒装。

  • Raising pets can cultivate the loving heart fo kids although they might be injurious to their familier or neighbors.

方式状语从句(好像,似乎)

引导词

  1. 常规引导词（后面是句子） 好像.似乎
  2. 介词短语 eg:
    She looks as if she were made of ice.

条件状语从句

引导词

都翻译成如果
eg:
As long as there seems a ghost of hope, i will never give up.

比较状语从句

引导词

you are as beautiful as i am beautiful.(后面的am beautiful省略)

写作的使用

作文中任何一句话的旁边都可以给他加一个状语的成分把句子拉长。

  • I had loved you deeply since i met you.
  • I love you for my whole life.
  • I love you in my deep mind.
  • I love you although you not make out the romance at all times.
