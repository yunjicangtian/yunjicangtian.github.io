强调句型

  • 强调句型可以强调除谓语之外的所有成分。
  • 跟过去式有关系都写成was，其它则is
    eg:
    我昨天在街上遇到了我的旧情人。
  • I met my old flame in the street yesterday.
  • It was i that my old flame in the street yesterday.
  • It was yesterday that i met my old flame in the street.
  • It is to realize my dream that i am considering weather l should depart with my boy friend.
  • There remain abundant element to account for the issue.

    写作

  • 作文中所有句子都可以写成强调句型。
  • So far, you have never realized that because you had unfavorable friends,you falled to go to your dreamful universty
  • So far, you have never realized that it was because you had unfavorable friends that you falled to go to your dreamful university.

倒装

什么是倒装？

倒装就是把一句话写成一般疑问句的形式，也就是给一句话价格助动词或吧be动词放在句首。

倒装用法

否定句放句首用倒装

  • I can handly speak Japanese.
  • Handly can i speak Japanese.
  • By no means do i love you.

虚拟语气

  • 虚拟语气表示与事实相反或语气委婉。

    一般用法

  • if引导的条件状语从句
  • If you are a tear in my eyes, i will never shed it
  • If i was a dog last night, i could sleep with you.
  • 那如何把一个条件状语从句变成虚拟语气呢？
  • 句子时态往后退一步。并且在be动词的过去式只有were
  • could=would=might

    特殊用法

  • 以下情况用”should + v”表示虚拟，并且should可以省略。
    以防：in case, lest, for fear that
    命：order, direct, command
    贱：advise, suggest, propose
    要求：ask, request, demand, require
    用虚拟
    是必要的：It is necessary/ipmortant that
  • In case that you should develop fatal infections, you had better stay at home.

    情态动词

  • 表明说话人主观态度的词汇。
  • 情态动词后跟动词原形
  • 情态动词就相当于助动词
  1. 情态动词表示对现在和将来的推测
  • He must have been self-biased in his university.（一定做过某事）
    否定：can’t have down
  • You need not have shown your love in this way.（本没必要做某事但是做了）
  • I could have became a prominent poet.（本能够做某事但是没有做,表示遗憾）
  • We should have got married if there exists a chance.（本应该做却没有做）

地点状语从句

常用引导词：where

  • The kind of trees grow well in moist places. in moist places = where it is moist
  • 做地点状语的通常都是介词短语，很少是从句。

原因状语从句

  1. 常规引导词
  2. 连词
  3. 介词短语

eg:

  • He had a car accident because he was carless.(状语随便放)
  • He had a car accident for was careless.(连词必须放中间，放前面或后面和空气连吗？)
  • He had a car accident because of his carelessness.(介词后面必须跟名词)

目的状语从句

  1. 动词不定式 动词不定式表目的，后面跟动词原形
  2. 引导词
  • We will ascend higher in order to overlook farther.(动词不定式表目的)
  • further 表示抽象的远，比如心与心之间的距离远。
  • In order that we overlook farther we will ascend higher.(状语从句引导词)

结果状语从句

很简单，和汉语语序完全一样

eg:
It feels so hot today that i wear my dress.
My mother proves so kind that se is respected by all her staff.

很有用

  • so…that(so是副词，后跟形容词)=very
  • 只要在作文中见到名词，都可以有意识的加上同位语.

让步状语从句(虽然…尽管)

一种委婉的语气，话不说满，往后退一步

引导词

while

三个意思：

  • 翻译为但是，放句中。

  • 翻译为当…时候，后面必须跟进行时态。

    as的部分倒装

  • As i seem ugly, i keep gentle.

  • Ugly i seem, i keep gentel.
    as 引导的让步状语从句，从句的表语可以放在句首构成部分倒装。

  • Raising pets can cultivate the loving heart fo kids although they might be injurious to their familier or neighbors.

方式状语从句(好像,似乎)

引导词

  1. 常规引导词（后面是句子） 好像.似乎
  2. 介词短语 eg:
    She looks as if she were made of ice.

条件状语从句

引导词

都翻译成如果
eg:
As long as there seems a ghost of hope, i will never give up.

比较状语从句

引导词

you are as beautiful as i am beautiful.(后面的am beautiful省略)

写作的使用

作文中任何一句话的旁边都可以给他加一个状语的成分把句子拉长。

  • I had loved you deeply since i met you.
  • I love you for my whole life.
  • I love you in my deep mind.
  • I love you although you not make out the romance at all times.

定语从句

什么是定语？

…的+名词。(..的)

定于的成分

  1. 形容词修饰名词
  • The innocent nightingale died.
  • The nightingale brave and innocent died.
  1. 名词修饰名词
  • The singing of the nightingale can make the rose bloom.
  1. 介词短语修饰名词
  • The nightingale out of the window heard the sighs of the youngster.
  1. 非谓语动词修饰名词
  • The singing nightingale lost his life.
  1. 从句修饰名词

  • 定语的位置

    短前长后
  • 当一个单词修饰名词的时候，定语放前面。
  • 当多个单词修饰名词的时候，定语放后面。
    This is a fiction about a nightingale and a rose.
    The youngster grasping romance left the party of the prince.
    The rejected boy looks pitiful.(当过去分词修饰名词时，即使只有一个单词也放在名词后面。)
    I have something important to tell you.(当形容词修饰不定代词的时候，即使只有一个单词也放在名词后。)

    定语从句的构成

    名词(先行词)+引导词+句子

    定语从句的引导词

    定语从句的引导词是按先行词的种类分的类，共5类：
  • 人：who whom whose
  • 物：that which whose
  • 时间：that which when
  • 地点：that which where
  • 原因：that which why
    eg:
    I will never foget the day when l met you.(when 是副词，不做从句中的主宾)
    I will never foget the day that we spent.(that 是动词，做从句中的主宾)
    You had better some reason why you are late.
    You ad better have some reason that sounds pefect. new 分类:
    按引导词本身的词性分类，3类：
  • 代词（在从句中一定要充当主语或宾语）：who whom that which
  • 副词（在从句中不能充当任何主干成分）：
    where when why
  • 形容词（在从句中修饰离他最近的那个名词）：
    whose

  1. Buildings whose roof are round look berutiful.
  2. It has been validated by numerous facts that glories belong to those whih dreams.

  3. that 作宾语可省略，但自己写作时不要省略。

并列句

  • 平行: and, not only…but also..

  • 转折: but, yet, while, whereas

  • 选择: or

  • 因果: for, so(有1无2)

  • 递进: then

    逻辑关系词

    只要上下句之间有逻辑关系，就一定要有逻辑关系词
    逻辑关系词(连，副，介词短语)

  • 平行: similarly, equally, likewise, at the same time,in the meanwhile

  • 转折: however, nevertheless, on the contrary, conversely, unexpectedly, unfortunately, by contrast

  • 选择: alternatively

  • 因果:therefore, thus, consequently, as a result

  • 递进: besides, furthermore, moreover, additionally, subsquently, in addition

    连词好其它逻辑关系词的区别在于

    连词的前面有无句号均可，而其他逻辑关系词前面，adding neither 句号,or and.
